# PakimonGO Product Concept

## Summary

PakimonGO is a mobile app where users photograph real-life animals, earn surprise server-scored points, build collections, share posts, explore animal activity on a map, and compete through leaderboards.

## Key Loops

- Capture animal photo.
- Submit context and visibility.
- Receive AI-assisted server score.
- Save to collection.
- Share with chosen audience.
- Compete on global, country, local, and friends leaderboards.

## Safety Rule

The app must reward safe, respectful observation and avoid encouraging harassment, risky petting, zoo farming, duplicate farming, or exact-location exposure.
